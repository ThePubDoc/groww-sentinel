## Phase Goal

**As** the portfolio owner, **I want** Friday's digest to append a weekly recap, **so that**
I get a once-a-week summary of movers, value change, and how many flags fired — without
opening the app. This is the final telemetry slice.

<objective>
Append a Friday-only weekly summary block (PNL-05): best/worst 2-3 movers by % price change
over the trading week, the week's portfolio value change, and the flags-fired count (sum of
stored daily flags_fired). Per D-08 movers come from per-symbol snapshot history and the
week baseline is the earliest snapshot in the current ISO week (Monday-of-today). Friday is
detected via the injected IST `today` (today.weekday() == 4, D-06) — the pure helpers take
`today` and never read a clock.

How many movers (2 vs 3) and the block layout are Claude's discretion per D-08/02-CONTEXT.
The block is omitted when the week has fewer than 2 snapshot days (02-RESEARCH Pattern 5).

Purpose: the weekly recap is the "Friday digest also carries a weekly summary" promise from
the project brief. Output: pure weekly-math helpers, a notify weekly block, and a Friday
gate in sentinel.

## Artifacts this plan produces
- state.py: `week_start(today)`, `weekly_movers(snapshots, today, top_n=3)`, `week_value_change(snapshots, today)`, `flags_fired_this_week(snapshots, today)` pure helpers.
- notify.py: weekly block rendering (fed an optional `weekly` dict).
- sentinel.py: Friday detection gating the weekly computation + wiring.
</objective>
